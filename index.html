Privacy Policy

App: Eventio  |  Developer: MARA Studios  |  Effective date: April 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how MARA Studios ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the Eventio mobile application ("App"). By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: When you sign in with Google or Apple, we receive your email address and a unique user identifier.
  • Event data: Titles, dates, types, and settings of the personal events you create, stored in your account.
  • Profile images: If you choose to upload a background image for an event card, the image is stored securely in Firebase Cloud Storage.
  • Device information: We collect an FCM (Firebase Cloud Messaging) token to send you push notifications.
  • Purchase information: If you subscribe to Eventio Pro, purchase transactions are handled by Google Play or the Apple App Store and RevenueCat. We do not store your payment details.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and maintain your account
  • To store and sync your personal events across devices
  • To send push notifications for your events (only if you enable them)
  • To process and verify your premium subscription status
  • To display relevant advertisements to free-tier users via Google AdMob
  • To improve the App's performance and user experience

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services, each with their own privacy practices:

  • Firebase (Google LLC) — Authentication, database, cloud storage, and push notifications. Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat — Subscription and purchase management. Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob — Advertising for free-tier users. Ads may use your device's advertising ID. Privacy Policy
  • Google Sign-In / Sign in with Apple — Authentication only; we do not access your contacts, calendar, or other account data.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. Data is only shared with the third-party services listed above to the extent necessary to operate the App.

5. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your event data and profile information will be permanently deleted from our servers within 30 days.

6. Children's Privacy

Eventio is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Opt out of personalized advertising (via your device settings)
  • Withdraw consent at any time by deleting your account

8. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including Firebase's built-in encryption and security rules to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
